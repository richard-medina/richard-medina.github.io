Research Interests:

  • Analysis of PDEs
  • Well-posedness and long-time assymptotic analysis
  • Kinetic equations in bounded domains
  • Kinetic problems in non-equilibrium settings
  • kinetic Fokker-Planck, Boltzmann, Landau, and Vlasov-Poisson Boltzmann equations
  • Perturbation theory
  • Semigroup theory
  • Hypocoercivity theory
  • Krein-Rutmann and Doblin-Harris theorems
