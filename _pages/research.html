Research interests:

  • Analysis of PDEs
  • Well-posedness and long-time assymptotic analysis
  • Kinetic equations in bounded domains
  • kinetic Fokker-Planck equations, Boltzmann equation, Landau equation, Vlasov-Poisson Boltzmann equation
  • Hypocoercivity
  • Kinetic problems in non-equilibrium settings
  • Krein-Rutmann-Doblin-Harris theory
